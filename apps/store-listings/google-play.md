# Google Play Store Listing

## App Name
Veil — Ad Blocker & Privacy

## Short Description (80 chars)
Block ads & trackers system-wide via local VPN. No root. Fast. Private.

## Full Description
Veil is a system-wide ad and tracker blocker for Android. It works across all apps and browsers without requiring root access.

HOW IT WORKS:
The app creates a local VPN tunnel (your traffic never leaves the device). All DNS requests to advertising and tracking domains are blocked at the system level, providing protection across every app on your device.

⚡ PERFORMANCE:
• Powered by the same engine as our browser extension (1.7M req/s)
• Minimal battery impact — DNS-level filtering is lightweight
• No noticeable slowdown to your internet connection
• Fast startup with serialized engine cache

🚫 BLOCKS ADS EVERYWHERE:
• In-app ads across all applications
• Browser ads in Chrome, Firefox, Samsung Internet, and more
• Video pre-roll ads in supported apps
• Pop-ups and interstitials

🔒 PRIVACY PROTECTION:
• Blocks trackers and analytics across all apps
• Prevents fingerprinting attempts
• No data leaves your device
• No external servers — everything is processed locally

📋 FILTER LISTS:
• EasyList — comprehensive ad blocking
• EasyPrivacy — tracker and analytics blocking
• Peter Lowe's List — ad server domains
• AdGuard DNS filter — mobile-optimized blocking
• Regional lists for localized content
• Automatic updates to stay current

🧠 SMART FEATURES:
• Auto-learning engine discovers new ad patterns
• Per-app whitelist — disable blocking for specific apps
• Scheduling — set active hours for blocking
• Statistics — see how many requests are blocked daily
• Battery usage monitoring

🔧 ADVANCED:
• Custom DNS server support
• Import/export settings
• Custom block/allow rules
• Widget for quick toggle

PRIVACY COMMITMENT:
• All traffic is processed locally on your device
• No external VPN servers
• No data collection or analytics
• No accounts required
• Open source (MIT license)

IMPORTANT NOTE:
This app uses Android's VPN interface for local DNS filtering. Your traffic does NOT pass through any external servers. The VPN icon in your status bar indicates local filtering is active.
